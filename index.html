WHAT WE DO

Got an idea and need help making it a reality, or a product that needs improvements?

Undesigned builds awesome products from start to finish, or hones in on specific areas you want help with. We're a team of designers, developers, strategists, and more who love startups and want to help you build your dreams.

OUR PHILOSOPHIES

  1. Date, don't marry.

    You wouldn't marry a stranger, so why sign a long-term contract? We'll give you a flat weekly rate to provide you flexibility and peace of mind.

  2. KISS.

    Keep It Simple, Stupid. We hate complexity and will ruthlessly cut it out wherever we can. You can see this in our business model, our designs, and our interactions.

  3. Brains over beauty.

    Design is the process of solving problems. Good design is invisible. It doesn't "wow," it makes customers say "of course." In fact, the best designs almost seem undesigned.

  4. Don't be a gold digger.

    We're passionate about solving problems, and we want to help you kick ass. We will always value honesty over making a quick buck.

  5. No strings attached.

    We only work on projects that will actually benefit from our services. Get in touch and we'll help you figure out what's best for your project.
