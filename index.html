OUR PHILOSOPHIES

  1. Date, don't marry.

    You wouldn't marry a stranger, so why sign a risky long-term contract with a random design consultancy? Take us on a date, and see what we can do for you in 7 days.

  2. KISS.

    Keep It Simple, Stupid. We hate complexity and will ruthlessly cut it out wherever we can. You can see this in our business model, our designs, and our interactions.

  3. Be more than a pretty face.

    Good design is invisible. It doesn't "wow," it makes customers say "of course." In fact, the best designs almost seem undesigned.

  4. Love trumps all.

    We are passionate about our work and aren't happy until we've made something that customers love. Love is a strong emotion, but so is passion.
